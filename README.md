# Bloom Burst: A Floral Puzzle Game

![Bloom Burst Gameplay Screenshot Placeholder](assets/screenshot_placeholder.png)  <!-- Replace with a real screenshot -->

## Description

Bloom Burst is a relaxing yet challenging puzzle game where you cultivate and arrange vibrant floral arrangements to fulfill specific orders. Drag and drop different flower types onto a grid, strategically rotating and placing them to maximize bloom potential and meet criteria like color combinations, flower sizes, and arrangement symmetry. As you progress, you'll unlock new flower species, tools, and decorative elements to create increasingly complex and stunning floral masterpieces. The game features a zen mode for relaxed play and a challenge mode with timed orders and scoring multipliers.

## Features

*   **Intuitive Drag-and-Drop Interface:** Easily place and rotate flowers on the grid.
*   **Diverse Flower Selection:** Unlock and utilize a wide variety of flower species, each with unique characteristics.
*   **Challenging Puzzle Mechanics:** Strategically arrange flowers to meet specific order requirements, including:
    *   Color Combinations
    *   Flower Sizes
    *   Arrangement Symmetry
*   **Zen Mode:** Relax and create beautiful arrangements without time pressure or score constraints.
*   **Challenge Mode:** Test your skills with timed orders and scoring multipliers for a competitive experience.
*   **Progression System:** Unlock new flowers, tools, and decorative elements as you progress through the game.
*   **Stunning Visuals:** Enjoy vibrant and detailed graphics that bring your floral creations to life.
